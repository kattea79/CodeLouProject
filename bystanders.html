The Battle of Mini and Moonpie

Innocent Bystanders (or are they?)

As you can guess Mini and Moonpie are not the only household residents. How do their actions impact those around them?

John

No cat is an island and our intrepid warriors are no exception. As a part time chauffeur, chamber-pot emptier, fur collector, and cut-man, John fills an important support role in the war effort.

A permanent resident of the battle ravaged land he often finds himself in the path of destruction yet tries to stay out of the way.

Tracy

As chief chef, groomer and trampoline, Tracy spends her days wafting between fear and friendliness. She often tries to keep the peace in the house, yet knows this is all in vein. Deep down she is rooting for Mini though (shhh don't tell Moonpie).

Many nights when Tracy is on the brink of sleep, Moonpie decides to wake from her slumber and drive her sharp talons into Tracy's flesh while launching off of the bed. Is anyone really surprised Tracy is championing Mini?
