The Battle of Mini and Moonpie

The Warriors

If you toss and turn at night asking yourself, 'Who are these crazy cats?' then this is the place for you!

Mini

Mini is an 11.5" tall mini panther. She is extremely friendly and loving yet skittish. Belly rubs are one of her favorite things.

After years of being attacked by Moonpie when she is at her most vulnerable she tends to run as soon as Moonpie enters the room. Occasionally the balance of power shifts and Mini recognizes she is the bigger cat and attacks back.

Moonpie

Moonpie, although a slightly smaller and trimmer cat standing at 11" tall looks a lot like her sister. She is also quite friendly yet more needy and always underfoot.

Normally the more dominant cat Moonpie tends to be more aggressive and is extremely surprised when the tide turns.
